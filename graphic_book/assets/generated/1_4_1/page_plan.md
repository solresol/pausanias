# Passage 1.4.1 Page Plan

- Passage ID: 1.4.1
- Key places/events/objects: Galatai/Keltoi at the far edge of Europe; the unnavigable outer sea with tides and strange beasts; the Eridanos/Rhine and the Phaethon myth; the invasion route through Illyria, Macedonia, Thessaly, and Thermopylae.
- Main visual: public-domain antique Europe atlas raster from Wikimedia Commons, warmed into the graphic-book palette, then overlaid locally with route arrows, sea-beast/tide details, and exact Pausanias labels.
- Insets: sourced raster seashore/tide scene for the outer-ocean shore; purpose-generated non-nude Eridanos/Heliades riverbank scene showing mourning daughters, poplars, amber tears, and distant Phaethon; compact antique-map route callout naming Illyria, Macedonia, Thessaly, and Thermopylae.
- Orientation strategy: label the outer sea, Galatai/Keltoi, Eridanos/Rhine, Ionian Sea, Illyria, Macedonia, Thessaly, and Thermopylae; connect callouts with leaders to the relevant terrain and route points.
- Approved reference pages: graphic_book/images/1/1/4.png and graphic_book/images/1/1/5.png for parchment atlas grammar, panels, label cards, leaders, and callout rhythm.
- Sourced art base: graphic_book/assets/generated/1_4_1/europe_1000_source.jpg, downloaded from Wikimedia Commons `File:Europe 1000.jpg` and used as a historic atlas texture/orientation layer rather than as a chronological claim.
- Sourced scenic inset: graphic_book/assets/generated/1_4_1/seashore_source.jpg, downloaded from Wikimedia Commons `File:Goyen 1645 Seashore at Scheveningen.jpg` and used for tide/outer-sea atmosphere.
- Generated scenic inset: graphic_book/assets/generated/1_4_1/eridanos_heliades_generated.png, generated specifically for this passage as a non-nude Eridanos/Phaethon/Heliades scene rather than copied unrelated art.
- Content suitability: source images and final crops for this page were checked for visible nudity. The earlier Phaethon engraving source was rejected because it introduced nude figures into the final panel; the unrelated poplars-only source was rejected because it failed semantic fit.
