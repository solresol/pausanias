# Passage 1.3.5 Page Plan

- Passage ID: 1.3.5.
- Key places/events/objects: sanctuary of the Mother of the Gods; Council Chamber of the Five Hundred; Zeus Boulaios; Apollo by Peisias; Demos by Lyson; Protogenes' painting of the Thesmothetai; Olbiades' painting of Callippos leading Athenians at Thermopylae against the Gauls.
- Main visual: a rich painterly reconstruction of the west Agora civic precinct, using the existing high-quality Agora reconstruction as the raster base and recontextualizing it around the Metroon / Bouleuterion / Council Chamber zone.
- Insets: a deterministic civic-precinct locator map; a statue-and-council object study for Zeus Boulaios, Apollo, and Demos; a painted-tablet Thermopylae reference for Callippos and the Galatian invasion.
- Orientation strategy: keep the main image architectural and spatial, label the sanctuary, Council Chamber, statue group, and Agora, then use the locator map and Thermopylae inset to separate local topography from the later historical painting named by Pausanias.
