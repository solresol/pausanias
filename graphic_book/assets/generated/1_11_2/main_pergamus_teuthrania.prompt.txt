Style/medium: sophisticated painterly historical illustration, watercolor and gouache over parchment, archaeological reconstruction, coffee-table atlas quality; comparable in ambition to a richly illustrated historical atlas page, not a comic or diagram.
Composition/framing: wide landscape view, 16:9, high-detail city approach; foreground figures small enough that the city/topography dominates. Leave clean areas in sky and land where local labels can be overlaid later.
Lighting/mood: warm late afternoon Mediterranean light, atmospheric mountains, realistic stone, bronze, dust, cypress and scrub.
Color palette: parchment ochres, muted greens, stone, bronze, blue-gray distant hills; refined, varied, not monochrome.
Text: none.
Constraints: no written labels, no inscriptions, no maps, no modern objects, no visible nudity, no eroticized poses, no gore, no cartoon styling, no flat vector terrain, no classroom diagram, no watermark.
